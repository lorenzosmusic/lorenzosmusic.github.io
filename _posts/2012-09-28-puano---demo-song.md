We've got a single we wrote called <b>Puano</b> that we're really excited about and wanted to share it with you.<br />
<br />
This song started one night while Mark and I were setting up in the back studio. There's a piano in the room and Mark sat down and started playing what became the lead line of the song. While he was playing I got behind some drums and came up for the rhythm for it.<br />
<br />
Luckily, we had the recorder running and were able to get the whole thing down. As far as the song name- it's called <b>Puano</b> because when we titled the file we recorded, we were just going to call it <i>"Piano"</i> to remember it. But I mistyped the word. When we noticed this later we thought it was kinda funny and kept it.<br />
<br />
This is an exclusive because it's a demo version of the song. It may change over time and you can have a copy that may not even be the version we release. It could change dramatically!<br />
<br />
