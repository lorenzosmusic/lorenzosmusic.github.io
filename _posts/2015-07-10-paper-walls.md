Song was a creation in three parts and that's how it sounded. Part 1, part 2 then part 3 and so on. Never felt right so we said we'd come back to it. Never did. Then last week it was found again. </div>
<div dir="ltr">
<br /></div>
<div dir="ltr">
Learning from recent single releases and cover songs, able to look at it in new light. And last night made a breakthrough. Super excited about it. But not finished yet.<br />
<br /></div>
<div dir="ltr">
The greatest part is the creation. We covered the studio walls with paper from an industrial roll that Rob had found and started just writing ideas, structure &amp; lyrics all over the walls.<br />
<br /></div>
<div dir="ltr">
The hardest thing to decide is which to focus on first. Its become more contest to see what will win.<br />
