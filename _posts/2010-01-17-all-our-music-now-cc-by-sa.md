<a href="http://creativecommons.org/images/deed/cc-logo.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://creativecommons.org/images/deed/cc-logo.jpg" /></a></div>
Sure, we've had our music licensed as <a href="http://creativecommons.org/">Creative Commons</a> for quite some time now. But we've mainly used the non-commercial one. This version meant you could only build upon our work non-commercially.<br />
<br />
Now under the <a href="http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/">Attribution Share Alike license</a> or <i>CC-BY-SA</i>, you can remix, tweak, and build upon our music even for commercial reasons, as long as is credited to us and licensed under the <b>identical</b> terms.<br />
<br />
This probably means more to us then it does to you. But it also means you can <b>share our music</b> with anyone for free and not hurt our feelings. :)<br />
<br />
As a matter of fact we're going to help you do that right now:<br />
<ol>
<li>Choose one of the albums below.</li>
<li>Then on the album's page click the "<b>download</b>" link.</li>
<li>In the pop-up window enter "0" in the price field - <i>or you could donate an amount if you would like.</i> :D</li>
<li>Enter you friends email and click "<b>submit</b>".</li>
</ol>
An email will be sent to your friend with a link for the download.<br />
Although you may want to let them know you did this so they don't get confused.<br />
