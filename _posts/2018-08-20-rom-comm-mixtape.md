<p style="text-align: left;"><b>Rom-Comm Mixtape</b> is a new experimental artistic venture. A collection of songs and sounds are a personal expression of life and tribulation.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><b>The project was created using nothing but open-source software and tools,</b>&nbsp;right down to the operating system on my&nbsp;laptop, <a href="https://ubuntustudio.org/" target="_blank">Ubuntu Studio</a>. It's also released under a creative commons license, which means it is free to use for your own projects.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">It's a feeling that takes me back to the origins of creating music I remember. When you come up with an idea and build on it to capture a feeling as you go.</p>
