We recently started work on a new rehearsal/recording room.<br />
<br />
This is the spot where we recorded the <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LWeuSNTGHtw" target="_blank">video for our song "Picture it all"</a>.<br />
<br />
<!--more--><br />
<br />
About a year ago, we had used this spot to try out an experiment-- to make a mobile electronic headphone based set up. Never really panned out.<br />
<br />
This past month, while working on a different way to streamline our recording process, we started to brainstorm and just started building a new room.<br />
<br />

Our plan is for this not only be a place to directly record practices, we will also be working towards a live set up for broadcasting shows.<br />
<br />
We hope to have more to share as this moves forward.<br />
