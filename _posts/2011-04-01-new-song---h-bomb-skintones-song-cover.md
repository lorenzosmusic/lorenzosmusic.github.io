An event where local <a class="zem_slink" href="http://maps.google.com/maps?ll=43.0666666667,-89.4&amp;spn=0.1,0.1&amp;q=43.0666666667,-89.4%20(Madison%2C%20Wisconsin)&amp;t=h" rel="geolocation" title="Madison, Wisconsin">Madison</a> bands played covers by other Madison bands.
