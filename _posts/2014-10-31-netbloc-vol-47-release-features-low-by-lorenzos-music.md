<h3>
Our single "Low" is on the blocSonic music compilation&nbsp;Grooves For The Sentient</h3>
<br />
<a href="http://www.blocsonic.com/releases/show/netbloc-vol-47--grooves-for-the-sentient" target="_blank">blocSonic</a>, a netlable that releases albums with music from around the globe under creative commons license has added us to it's latest edition. The release includes songs by&nbsp;<b>Cross My Heart Hope To Die</b>,&nbsp;<b>Valley Hush</b>,&nbsp;<b>Summer Of Haze</b>,&nbsp;<b>Miracles of Modern Science</b>&nbsp;&amp;&nbsp;<b>The Impossebulls</b>&nbsp;to name a few.<br />
<!--more--><br />
<blockquote class="tr_bq">
"With this series, blocSonic always sets out to hammer home the message that FREE music doesn’t equate with a lack of quality. The reasons artists release their art for free are varied, but ultimately, they choose to give it away so that it’s easier for you to hear it and, in the case of CC licensed music, to share it."</blockquote>
Previously, we were lucky enough to be part&nbsp;<a href="http://www.lorenzosmusic.com/2010/03/free-indie-music-compilation-netbloc.html" target="_blank">netBloc Vol. 27: Straight Outta The FMA</a>, another great compilation with the different types of music that the <a class="zem_slink" href="http://www.freemusicarchive.org/" rel="homepage" target="_blank" title="Free Music Archive">Free Music Archive</a> has to offer.<br />
<br />
Check out blocSonic's-&nbsp;<b>netBloc Vol. 47: Grooves For The Sentient</b> and enjoy all the music!<br />
