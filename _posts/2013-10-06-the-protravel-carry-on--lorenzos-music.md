We were sent this interesting link the other day.<br />
<br />
This is a <a href="http://goo.gl/TCN3Xh" target="_blank">Kickstarter campaign for a travel bag</a> that, in and of itself, seems like a pretty cool bag. And on top of that, with 10 days left at the time of posting this, they are well above the goal they're trying to reach to get these bags made.<br />
<br />
All this was cool, but we were still wondering what this was sent to us for? It couldn't just be for our worldly exports.<br />
<br />
<!--more--><br />
Then we played the promotional video and were happy to see that they had used our song "<a href="http://goo.gl/Iph4Oe">Feed The One</a>" from the album <a href="http://goo.gl/XFgXfk">BAZAR</a>&nbsp;as the background music!<br />
<br />
<iframe frameborder="0" height="400" src="http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/minaal/protravel-carry-on-travel-faster-happier-and-more/widget/video.html" width="520"> </iframe>

Always love it when we find out we've become a part of something.<br />
<br />
Good luck to <b>Minaal </b>and their ProTravel Carry-On.<br />
