Years ago, A friend of Mark's named <b>Jeremy Keller</b>, came into town.<br />
He was hitchhiking around the country and living in a tent. When he passed through Mark asked him to manage the band for a while.<br />
<br />
Makes sense right?<br />
<br />
He was a mad man. In a good way, but crazy nonetheless.<br />
<br />
After a few short months of him managing us he decided to pack up his tent and go to Alaska.<br />
Never really knew why?<br />
<br />
<b>Fast forward to present day:</b><br />
He's still out there (in Alaska that is). And somehow, now has a spot on a new <b>Discovery Channel</b> reality series called "<a href="http://www.discovery.com/tv-shows/edge-of-alaska" target="_blank">Edge of Alaska</a>".<br />
