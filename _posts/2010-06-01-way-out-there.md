When I first got it I was like 'whoa', and then I was all like 'WTF', as in, which sound should I use?&nbsp; There is such a wide variety of tones available.&nbsp; Finally I think i settled in on a high gain creamy grit for the main distorted sound, and the rest of the tones I need I can get with foot pedals on the clean channel.<br /><br />&nbsp;This SG guitar is truly a treat.&nbsp; It seems to have riffs that just come out of it.&nbsp; Like they are trapped inside the guitar, and when I strap it on, I just release them.&nbsp; Waaay out dude?&nbsp; Not.&nbsp; Gotta see it to believe it.<br /><br />Better yet, the new EP, due this summer, has these riffs all over it.&nbsp; When it comes out, take a listen, and then you can decide and tell me if I'm <b>waaay out there, dude.</b>
