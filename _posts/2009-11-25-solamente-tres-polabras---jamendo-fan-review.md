<a href="http://jamendo.com/">Jamendo.com</a> is a great site filled with like-minded <b>Creative Commons</b> artists like us. It's a fan based community, a lot like <a href="http://last.fm/">last.fm</a> but for the artist. You control your musical rights and&nbsp;distribution. But more importantly, it's fan based.<br />
We've been on <b>Jamendo</b> for over a year and are still getting great comments on our albums. Like this one for our 3rd release <b><a href="http://lorenzosmusic.bandcamp.com/album/solamente-tres-palabras">Solamente Tres Polabras</a></b>.<br />
